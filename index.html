Content Tips

  1. Objectives - not needed
  2. Put your education first
  3. Keep it to a single page
  4. GPA < 3.0 - leave it off
  5. Use action words
  6. Focus on outcomes

Education

  • Bachelor of Science / Bachelor of Arts
  • Expected Graduation
  • Major
  • Minor(s)
  • GPA
  • Coursework: don't put it down unless your resume is really empty
